Ros√© Chicken Pasta

  • 500g Penne
  • Lots of Butter
  • $7 pack Chicken Breast
  • 1 big Onion
  • Half a $1 pack Celery
  • 2 Tbps Garlic
  • 1 jar Rose Sauce
  • Decent amount Thyme, Oregano, Salt, Pepper
  • 2 Handfuls Spinach
  • As needed Olive Oil
  • A bit of Green Pepper
  • Garnish with Spring Onions

    Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into small cubes, marinate at least 1 hour in Olive Oil and assorted spices
  2. Dice the onions and celery, really small
  3. In an appropriately sized pot, cook the chicken. If it sticks add more olive oil.
  4. Once mostly cooked, take it outta there, put the Butter, Onion, Celery in, like 5 minutes. Don't leave!
  5. When onions smell really good, add Garlic and Peppers, cook 30 seconds.
  6. Add remaining spices. Stire for a few seconds. Add Sauce. Give it a minute. Taste. If not ideal add more spice.
  7. Add more salt, you probably under-did it.
  8. When it starts to bubble turn the heat down a bunch. Boil water on another burner. Add salt to water. Cook Pasta.
  9. Check Pasta in 8 minutes. Eat one. If not good, wait a minute and repeat until good.
  10. Its good? Drain, throw in spinach while its still hot.
  11. Put Pasta in bowl, put sauce on top. Cut up some Spring Onions and sprinkle them on top. Serve.
